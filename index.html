Who We Are

Valued as Gold,
Clear as Water

Named after the golden Sunkoshi River flowing from the Himalayan range of Mount Everest, our company reflects its namesake — valued as gold, clear and transparent as water, and enduring as a river in flow.

Since 1995, we have deployed thousands of skilled Nepali workers to Gulf countries, Malaysia, and Japan. Situated at the heart of Kathmandu — just 3.5 km from Tribhuvan International Airport — we offer fast and efficient processing. Our sister institutions, Sunkoshi Training Centre and Koshi Tours & Travel, complete our end-to-end service offering.

How It Works

Our Recruitment Process

A transparent, step-by-step journey from application to deployment — built on 30 years of experience.

1
Apply & Register

Submit your CV and details. Our team pre-screens all applicants against job requirements.

2
Interview & Test

Shortlisted candidates attend final interviews with employer delegates and trade tests.

3
Medical & Visa

Medical checkup, document legalization, ministry approvals, and visa processing by the employer.

4
Fly & Deploy

Orientation completed. Workers depart with full documentation. Employer notified 48 hours in advance.
