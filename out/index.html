How It Works

Our Recruitment Process

A transparent, step-by-step journey from application to deployment.

1
Apply & Register

Submit your CV. Our team pre-screens all applicants against job requirements.

2
Interview & Test

Shortlisted candidates attend final interviews with employer delegates and trade tests.

3
Medical & Visa

Medical checkup, document legalization, ministry approvals, and visa processing.

4
Fly & Deploy

Orientation completed. Workers depart with full documentation. Employer notified 48 hours prior.
