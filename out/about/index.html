About Sunkoshi Manpower

30 years of trust, transparency, and transforming lives through international employment.

1995

Our Story

Valued as Gold,
Clear as Water

Named after the golden Sunkoshi River — a river flowing from the Himalayan range of Mount Everest — our company reflects its namesake: valued as gold, clear and transparent as water, and enduring as a river in flow.

Since 1995, Sunkoshi Manpower Service (P.) Ltd. has been dedicated to connecting skilled Nepali workers with reputable employers in the Gulf countries, Malaysia, and Japan. We are situated in the heart of Kathmandu — just 3.5 km from Tribhuvan International Airport — enabling fast and efficient processing.

The company believes in mutual trust and benefits, focusing on transparent communication, quality candidate assessment, and timely deployment. Our sister institutions — Sunkoshi Training Centre and Koshi Tours & Travel — complete our end-to-end service offering.
